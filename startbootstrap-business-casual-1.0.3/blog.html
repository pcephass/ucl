As of December 9, 2015 Spanish Club Real Madrid has taken part in the most UEFA Champions League finals with 13 apperances. Real Madrid has also won the most Champions League finals with a total of 10 championships and have won the most consecutive championship runs. They won the 1956,1957,1958,1959 and 1960 seasons.

Italian Club Juventus have lost the most in the fianls with 6 times as runner-ups. German club Bayern Munich and Portugese club SL Benfica come in at a close second, with 5 times as runner-up each.
